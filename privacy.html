Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your information.

Last updated: August 28, 2023

Introduction

Welcome to AmbigramGen ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information about you when you use our ambigram generator service.

By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Don't Collect

We want to emphasize that we do not collect or store any of the following:

  • Any text you input into the ambigram generator
  • The ambigrams you create using our service
  • Personal identification information unless you voluntarily provide it (e.g., through contact forms)
  • Your IP address or location data

Information We May Collect

While using our service, we may collect the following types of information for analytics and service improvement purposes:

  • Aggregate usage data (e.g., number of visitors, which features are used most often)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referral source
  • Pages visited on our site
  • Time spent on each page

This information is collected anonymously and cannot be used to identify you personally.

How We Use Collected Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Improve and optimize our website and service
  • Understand how users interact with our generator
  • Identify and fix technical issues
  • Add new features that users will find valuable

Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies - small text files placed on your device - to enhance your experience. These cookies:

  • Remember your font and color preferences
  • Track usage patterns to improve our service
  • Do not collect personal information

You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though this may affect the functionality of our website.

Third-Party Services

We may use third-party services to help us operate our website and analyze usage. These third parties have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Analytics: For website traffic analysis
  • Font providers: To deliver font styles for your ambigrams

These third parties are prohibited from using your information for any purpose other than providing services to us.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect any information collected through our website. However, since your ambigram creations are processed locally in your browser and not stored on our servers, there is no risk of your designs being accessed by unauthorized parties through our systems.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
