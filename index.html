Everything you need to know about our ambigram generator

An ambigram is a typographical design that may be read as one or more words when viewed from different orientations, typically rotated 180 degrees. Some ambigrams can also be read as different words depending on the viewing direction, creating an intriguing visual effect perfect for tattoos, logos, and artistic expressions.

Our generator allows you to download your ambigrams in high-resolution PNG format, which is ideal for most uses including digital displays and printing. Not all words work perfectly as ambigrams. Success depends on letter shapes that can transform into readable characters when rotated. Some letters (b/q, d/p, m/w) work better than others. Try different fonts, as some are designed to work better for ambigrams. Also, shorter words generally produce better results than longer ones.

Here are some helpful tips for creating better ambigrams:

  • Choose words with similar letter shapes when rotated (e.g., b/q, d/p, m/w)
  • Try one-word ambigrams that read the same when rotated
  • Experiment with different fonts - some work better than others for specific words
  • Keep words relatively short (2-8 letters)
  • Use the color gradient feature to enhance the visual effect
